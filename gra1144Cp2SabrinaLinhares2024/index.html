Sweet Gabby Brigadeiros

Brigadeiros

We all love Sweet Gabby’s Brigadeiros, and have her secrets in our hands. The best part is, we are sharing it with you! We will help you step by step, ready? Let’s do it! 😋

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 spoons of chocolate powder or cocoa.
  • ½ spoon of butter
  • 200g of chocolate sprinkles

Steps:

  1. In a medium size saucepan, pour out the condensed milk, heavy cream and the chocolate. Stir it until it is all homogeneous.
  2. Bring the saucepan to the stove over medium heat.
  3. Add the butter to bring a beautiful shine to the brigadeiro.
  4. Keep stirring until the mix is not sticking in the pan anymore. One trick to know if it is ready, it is, slowly, lean the saucepan a little bit and everything comes out of the pan.
  5. Take it out of heat and let it cool in a plate and cover it with plastic wrap, this will keep the brigadeiro soft and creamy.
  6. Let it cool for at least 2 hours, or until you touch the bottom of the plate, and it is not hot anymore.
  7. The last step is like going back to your childhood and playing with playdough.
